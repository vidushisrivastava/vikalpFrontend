Vikalp provides you with an incredible panel of authenticated and verified mentors from different areas of expertise. Connect with them through chat, call or video call. We are more of a consultancy platform than a skill development platform. Initially we are starting with colleges, focusing on the areas where college students need the most amount of guidance. We conducted various surveys to identify these areas and the areas that we are starting with are competitive coding, soft skills(photography & graphic designing) and entrance exams(GRE & UPSC).
